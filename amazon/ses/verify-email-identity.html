(form)

Examples

Verify an email adress

This example shows how to verify an email address. Afterwards an email will be sent to the specified email adress where you have to click on a link to verify its existence. It replaces SES.VerifyEmailAddress.

Example

ses.VerifyEmailIdentity({EmailAddress:'anotheremail@domain.com'},function(err,data){
    fmt.dump(err, 'Error');
    fmt.dump(data, 'Data');
});
