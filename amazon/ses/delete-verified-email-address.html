Examples

(deprecated) Delete a verified email address

This example shows how to delete a verified email address only. Notice the difference with SES.DeleteIdentity that is used for unverified email addresses and/or domains.

Example

ses.DeleteVerifiedEmailAddress({ EmailAddress : 'email@domain.com' }, function(err, data) {
    fmt.dump(err, 'Error');
    fmt.dump(data, 'Data');
});
